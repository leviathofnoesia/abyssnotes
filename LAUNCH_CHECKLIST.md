## Pricing Decision

**Choose by end of Week 2:**

### Option A: One-Time Purchase
- Personal: $49
- Team: $149
- Enterprise: $499

**Pros:** Simple, no recurring billing for users
**Cons:** Lower LTV, need constant new sales

### Option B: Freemium
- Free: All core features
- Pro: $9/month or $79/year

**Pros:** Recurring revenue, higher LTV
**Cons:** Need more features to justify subscription

**Recommendation:** Start with Option A (one-time), test market response, consider freemium in v2.0.
